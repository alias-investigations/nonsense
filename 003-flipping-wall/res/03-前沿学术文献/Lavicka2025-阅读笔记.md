## 一、研究方法
- 分析CNKI数据库中1979-2022年被引用最多的200篇含"fazhi"或"yifazhiguo"关键词的论文
- 分三个时期：邓小平现代化时期(1980s-1999)、前习近平时期(2000-2012)、习近平时期(2013-2022)

## 二、核心发现

### 1. "中国特色社会主义法治"的本质
> "Adding the phrase 'with Chinese characteristics' to a generally accepted term, e.g. socialism, human rights, or democracy, signifies that the preceding term does not fully conform to its original meaning. Instead, it shows its subordination to Party-state objectives." (p.281)

> "The single term 'fazhi' in Chinese translates to both 'rule of law' and 'rule by law,' causing confusion and allowing the Chinese authorities room for flexible interpretation." (p.281)

### 2. 薄法治(Thin) vs 厚法治(Thick)
- 中国选择性采纳薄法治元素（程序性合法性），同时避免厚法治元素（司法独立、权利保护）
- 薄法治可以是"window dressing"——形式合法性掩盖实质压迫
- 中国偏好薄法治因为：(1)避免挑战党的领导；(2)保留"法治"话语权

### 3. 三个时期的学术演变

**1980s-1999（乐观改革期）**：
- 约半数文章讨论限制行政权力
- 约1/5承认党在推进法治中的关键角色
- 有学者主张引入西方法律概念
- 整体乐观：法治最终可以实现

**2000-2012（现代化中国特色期）**：
- 党的角色讨论增加
- "中国特色"概念泛化——暗示独特方法但无精确定义
- 强世功提出"党治法治"(party-based rule of law)
- 党规与国法的模糊界限问题浮现

**2013-2022（习近平时期）**：
- 超过半数文章讨论党-国-法关系
- 党规被提升为核心制度工具
- 学术讨论空间收窄——2014年政策定调后，理论辩论减少
- CNKI发文量从2015年高峰下降到2000年代水平

### 4. 核心矛盾
> "China wants to be associated with the term 'rule of law'. On the other hand, it is not interested in some aspects often related to it, such as the separation of powers ensuring judicial independence, or law supremacy, especially concerning the Communist Party." (p.281)

### 5. 结论
> "Under Xi Jinping, the rule of law is not conceived as a rule of rules." (p.294)

> "China employs established legal terms like the rule of law to make itself more understandable to the international community, leveraging the common language of global discourse. However, this willingness to adopt familiar terminology does not necessarily translate to strict adherence to their original definitions." (p.294)

> "China's re-interpretation of established legal terms is unlikely to lead to a freer and more equitable society and will certainly not enhance the rule of law." (p.294)

## 三、与翻墙研究的关系

### 1. fazhi的双重含义
fazhi同时翻译为"rule of law"和"rule by law"——这正是翻墙执法的核心问题：中国到底是在"依法治国"(rule of law)还是"以法而治"(rule by law)？

### 2. 选择性采纳
中国选择性采纳薄法治元素——翻墙执法正是这种选择性采纳的体现：引用暂行规定第6条（形式合法性），但回避VPN≠其他信道的技术事实（实质正义）。

### 3. 学术空间收窄
2014年后学术讨论空间收窄——翻墙法律争议在学术界几乎无法公开讨论。

### 4. 强世功的"党治法治"
强世功(2015)提出"党治法治"概念——翻墙执法可能正是"党治法治"的实践：党的网络管理政策通过法律形式执行。

## 四、核心引文

> "Every rule-of-law state must rule by law, but not every rule-by-law state has the rule of law." (Smith 2019, p.48, 引自本文p.284)

> "China and other authoritarian countries prefer to use the thinner version of the rule of law as it still allows them to claim the 'rule of law' as their characteristic while retaining their undemocratic regimes." (Bedner 2018, pp.40-41, 引自本文p.284)

> "The rule of law's dependence on political power is its greatest enemy." (Chen 2013, p.92, 引自本文p.290)

> "Party policy provides the guiding principles (the soul) for the law, while the law translates and legitimizes these policies (embodiment and legalization)." (Wang 2016, p.146, 引自本文p.290)

> "The CCP Central Committee holds a higher position within the constitutional system than the NPC." (Qiang 2015, p.21, 引自本文p.291)
