5GH Foundation is a non-profit organization for promoting science and technology, and is committed to allow the public to get benefit from these developments.

We focus on all issues related to the development of human societies. Through researches and global actions, we and our allies provide solid evidences for individuals, communities, and governments to make decisions.

In these researches and global actions, we and our allies provide a full range of support, including but not limited to consulting services, funding management, research facilities, and human/material resources.

With these efforts, we and our allies wish a better future of our human beings.
